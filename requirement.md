User Requirements Document: Cybersecurity Company Profile
1. Project Overview
The objective is to design and develop a professional, highly secure company profile website. The site must establish credibility, showcase technical expertise, and generate leads by clearly communicating the company's core cybersecurity services: Red Teaming, Security Awareness, Phishing Simulations, SOC Assessments, and Cybersecurity Outsourcing.

2. Target Audience
C-Level Executives (CISOs, CIOs, CTOs): Looking for strategic partnerships, SOC assessments, and high-level risk mitigation.

IT/Security Directors & Managers: Seeking specific services like Red Teaming or outsourcing to supplement their internal teams.

HR & Compliance Officers: Searching for employee security awareness training and phishing simulations.

3. Functional Requirements (Site Features & Pages)
3.1. Homepage
Hero Section: A clear, high-impact value proposition stating the company's mission in securing organizations against modern threats. Must include a prominent "Schedule a Consultation" Call-to-Action (CTA).

Service Matrix Overview: A scannable grid briefly introducing the five core services with "Learn More" links.

Trust Indicators: Sections for client logos, industry certifications (e.g., ISO 27001, CREST, OSCP), and brief testimonials.

3.2. Core Service Pages
Each service page must detail the methodology, deliverables, and business value.

Red Teaming: Explain the approach to simulating real-world adversaries, testing physical/digital boundaries, and delivering actionable technical reports.

Security Awareness: Highlight interactive training modules, compliance tracking, and the focus on building a human firewall.

Phishing Simulations: Describe the custom campaign creation, realistic decoy templates, and detailed metric reporting (click rates, compromise rates).

SOC Assessment: Detail the evaluation of current Security Operations Centers, gap analysis against industry standards (e.g., MITRE ATT&CK), and maturity improvement roadmaps.

Cybersecurity Outsourcing: Outline the process of providing skilled personnel (L1/L2 SOC Analysts, Penetration Testers, Security Engineers) to augment internal teams.

3.3. About Us & Credentials
Company Mission & Vision: The philosophy behind the firm's approach to cybersecurity.

Team Expertise: Highlighting the certifications and backgrounds of the core team (without exposing sensitive identities that could be targeted by social engineering).

3.4. Contact & Lead Generation
Secure Contact Form: Fields for Name, Company, Email, Role, and Service of Interest.

Lead Magnet Integration: A downloadable asset (e.g., "2026 Enterprise Phishing Threat Report" or a "Basic SOC Checklist") in exchange for an email address.

4. Non-Functional Requirements
Exceptional Security: As a cybersecurity firm, the website must be a fortress. It requires forced HTTPS (HSTS), strict Content Security Policies (CSP), protection against top OWASP vulnerabilities, and regular penetration testing before going live.

Performance & Speed: The site must load in under 2 seconds. The design should be modern but code-light, avoiding bloated frameworks that slow down performance.

Responsive Design: Flawless rendering across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

SEO Optimization: On-page technical SEO focusing on keywords like "Enterprise Red Team services," "SOC maturity assessment," and "Phishing simulation platform."

5. User Journey Example (The CISO Flow)
Entry: A CISO searches for "External SOC assessment services" and lands on the SOC Assessment service page via organic search.

Engagement: They read the methodology, view a sample anonymized deliverables report, and see the company's CREST certification.

Conversion: They click the "Request a Gap Analysis" CTA, fill out the secure intake form, and receive an automated, encrypted confirmation email.
